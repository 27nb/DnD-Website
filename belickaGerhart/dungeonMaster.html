Overview

Being the Dungeon Master, or DM, can be a very hard thing to do if you have no idea what you are doing. This is why it is a good idea to prepare before hand, making sure you have a set plan on where the characters need to go, and what they need to do while still leavin leverage for them to screw around and let everyone have fun.
